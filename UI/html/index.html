Online Elections Made Easy and Fast
Politico makes it possible to vote electronically online via smartphone, tablet or computer, from anywhere and at any time. This is done by creating a voter account and then use your credentials to log in to Politico. Voters can now cast a single vote, which is then stored and counted on the go. Results are available after all voters have completed voting.

Device independent
Politico can be used independently of platforms and devices, whether on your smartphone, tablet or computer.
Time- and location-independent
Voters can participate in elections and vote regardless of their location at the moment of election.
Accessibility
People with disabilities can take part in electronic voting and elections without assistance from others.
